Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our VR experiences. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us!

Do we need to book in advance for VR FUNTASTIC WORLD?

+

No, You don't need to book. The busiest times are weekends and holidays, so there maybe longer waiting times then.

Do you take group bookings?

+

Yes! Please email us with your preferences and we will be happy to accommodate you.

Can everyone do it?

+

Yes! However, we don't recommend it if you have heart problems, motion sickness, epilepsy or any such. We have customers from 5 years old to 75 years old.

Do we have to sign anything to have this awesome experience?

+

Everyone must sign a waiver for themselves or for their children who are under 18 years old.

Can we play Games or is it just rides?

+

Our concept includes games, rides and movies. We also have interactive shooting games. We have 2 kinds of rides where you sit in giant dinosaur eggs! There are lots of different experiences to choose from, suitable for everyone. Try our NEW double new egg ride for an even more exciting experience.

How long are the experiences?

+

3 minutes to 8 minutes depending on the experience you choose.

Where are you located?

+

We are located at KIDS MARKET in GRANVILLE ISLAND. On the second floor. We are open every day from 10-6pm

Can we come any day of the week day?

+

3rd party special deals only apply for weekdays. Weekends and holidays are the most popular times.

What are the games/rides?

+

Please check our website, you will see each experience explained. Also you can check out our Facebook and instagram account for more information and videos of people having fun in our store. #vrfuntasticworld
